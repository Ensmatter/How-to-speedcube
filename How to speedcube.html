This is a section on different speedcube brands.

                 

Company 1. Gan Cube

The gan flagships are a premium line of speedcubes that deliver top notch performance. Gan cubes feel light and smooth when turning and have a good range of settings. The only thing holding them back is their price which is as crazy as their performance.

     

Company 2. Moyu

Moyu has two very popular lines of speedcubes, number 1 being the wr m series which was also a very smooth cube that performed well however if you had sloppy execution of algorithims it would fall apart. The wrm cubes are risk reward cubes that are exceptional in the hands of experienced cubers. Moyus other line was the rs3m, which started of as a exceptional budget cube that once set up could rival premium flagships and has evolved into a slightly more expensive yet higher quality cube. Both lines of cubes are very good and worth their respective price tag.      

Company 3. X-man

X-man cubes have seen a lot of evolution since the first version. Now, the X-man v3 pioneer is essetially a gan cube but cheaper. Both the X-man v3 and Gan 13 have exceptional performance and the only highly noticable difference is that one feels like a X-man and the other feels like a Gan so it comes down to personal preference.

     

Those are the major cubes as of now, obviously there are more and some will be better then others depending on your preferences however any one of those cubes will get you fast times.
